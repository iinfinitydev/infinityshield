WHO WE ARE

Specialists in Passive Fire Protection Installation

Infinity Shield Technical Services LLC is the dedicated installation arm of the Infinity Shield group, delivering high-quality passive fire protection, fire stopping, and fire door installation across residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Backed by 20 years of industry experience from the United Kingdom, we combine skilled technicians, certified materials, and precise workmanship to ensure every installation meets the highest safety and compliance standards.

“Trusted installers of passive fire solutions that protect lives and property.”

WHAT WE DELIVER

Core Service Lines

Fire Stopping

Professional installation of fire stopping systems to maintain compartmentation integrity.

Cavity Barriers

Correct installation of cavity barriers in line with design specifications and regulations.

Compartmentation

Expert installation and verification of fire compartmentation systems.

Fire Doors Installation & Maintenance

Supply, installation, and ongoing maintenance of fire doors to the highest standards.

Steel Fire Protection

Application of intumescent coatings and fire protection systems for structural steel.

Digital Compliance Tracking

Value-added service providing transparent digital records of all installations and certifications.
